# NiveshNidhi

NiveshNidhi is a simple and trusted digital platform for chit funds.
It helps people join safe groups, track payments clearly, and get better confidence with AI and blockchain support.

Built with a hackathon mindset: practical, fast, and easy to understand.

## Vision

Make chit funds safer, more transparent, and easier for everyone, including first-time users.

## Key Features

### 1) Multilingual Experience
- Users can access important screens in multiple Indian languages
- Helpful for non-English speakers
- Improves adoption in Tier-2 and Tier-3 markets

### 2) Easy UI for Everyone
- Simple flow for signup, KYC, joining groups, and tracking payments
- Less confusion, fewer steps, clear actions
- Designed for users with low technical comfort

### 3) Location-Priority Group Listing
- Show nearby and relevant chit groups first
- City/state-based relevance improves trust and convenience
- Better matching for local communities

### 4) AI Chatbot Assistant
- In-app chatbot explains each step in plain language
- Helps with “How to join?”, “How payout works?”, “What is risk score?”
- Great for first-time users and demo judges

### 5) AI Risk Intelligence
- Flags risky profiles and risky group patterns
- Gives explainable suggestions for safer decisions
- Helps admins and users avoid bad actors

### 6) Blockchain Trust Layer
- Important financial events are hashed for tamper-evident proof
- Builds confidence that records were not secretly changed
- Useful for dispute resolution and trust building

### 7) Payment Microservices Approach
- Payment flow is split into focused services (order creation, contribution tracking, payout release, webhook handling)
- Better reliability and easier scaling during high traffic
- Faster debugging and safer upgrades for finance operations

## Typical User Journey

1. User creates account
2. Completes KYC verification
3. Browses local prioritized groups
4. Applies and joins a group
5. Pays contribution through secure flow
6. Tracks status and notifications in dashboard
7. Payout is released with risk checks and audit trail

## Algorithms We Will Use 

- Organizer risk score based on experience, license, and profile strength
- Group safety analysis with AI + fallback rules
- Payout risk gate to block suspicious releases
- Trust score ideas for future: behavior quality, payment discipline, and community signals
